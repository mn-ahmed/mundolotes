POS Multi Currency

Provide a multi-currency facility to your customers in POS!

Odoo POS Multi Currency Module enables POS users to accept payments in multiple currencies in POS stores. This plugin enables Multi Currency & Select Multiple Currencies in the Pos Configuration. Such currencies will then appear at the POS for the customers. The Added Payment line will be according to the selected currency.

Why provide a Multi-Currency option in POS?

Offering a multi-currency option will grant your customers the facility of shopping in their favorite currency.

Other than base currency, we can add multiple currencies for customer’s convenience.

Customers can make Payment in other currency also in the same Order.

Detailed Features List

Below is the detailed list of Feature for Pos Multi Currency

Accept payments in multiple currencies in POS

  • Odoo POS Multi Currency Module enables POS users to accept payments in multiple currencies in POS stores.
  • Allow the POS user to configure as many as currencies to the store.

Allows customers to shop in their preferred currency

  • Customers can select the currency of any country from the options listed in the POS.
  • Allow customers to checkout and pay in their selected currency.

Manage the multi currency settings from the Odoo backend

  • The admin can enable or disable the plugin.
  • You can add multiple currencies options from the Odoo backend.

Allow customers to pay in their preferred currency in POS

  • The customer can easily pay in their preferred currency on your POS store.
  • This enables you to retain your customers and make more profits.
